Intro

This is a personal page that I created to try my hand at blogging and technical writing. I like to tinker with tech and always had an interest in pushing the potential of older computers. I’m hoping to share share my experiences, side projects in tech that I’ve found fun and interesting.

Although I’ve created pages for internal clients during my day job, it is nice to build something that will be long living and share.

The influence for this page also comes from the Cloud Resume challenge by Forrest Brazeal.

Aside from sharing my findings but also sharing as documentation for my current setups.

My first set of posts will focus on the upgrades to my home lab infrastructure setup and current learnings.
